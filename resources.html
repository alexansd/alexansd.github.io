Crisis Lines & Immediate Help

Mental Health Crisis 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline Call or text 988 Free, confidential support for people in distress, 24/7. Also available at 988lifeline.org
Online Safety Emergency NCMEC CyberTipline 1-800-843-5678 Report online enticement, child exploitation, or suspected grooming to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children.
Bullying & Harassment Crisis Text Line Text HOME to 741741 Free 24/7 text-based mental health support for teens experiencing bullying, anxiety, or emotional distress.
Eating Disorders ANAD Helpline 1-888-375-7767 The National Alliance for Eating Disorders helpline, for families concerned about social media's impact on body image.

Mental Health & Wellbeing

American Psychological Association — Social Media Hub Research

The APA's dedicated collection of research, articles, and guidance on social media's psychological effects on children and teenagers.

↗
Child Mind Institute — Technology & Social Media Parent Guide

Practical, clinician-authored articles on anxiety, depression, and how digital life affects child and adolescent development.

↗
Common Sense Media — Social Media Guides Parent Tool

Age-based guidance on apps, platforms, and research-backed strategies for managing your child's social media use.

↗
ADAA — Anxiety & Depression Association of America Organisation

Find therapists, access screening tools, and read research on anxiety and depression in adolescents linked to social media use.

Screen Time & Digital Wellbeing

American Academy of Pediatrics — Media & Children Medical Guidance

Official AAP guidelines on screen time by age, with tools like the Family Media Plan to help create household agreements.

↗
HealthyChildren.org — Media & Technology Parent Resource

The AAP's parent-facing site with practical guides on managing screen time, gaming, and social media at every developmental stage.

↗
Sleep Foundation — Technology & Teen Sleep Research

Evidence-based guidance on how devices and social media disrupt adolescent sleep cycles, and what to do about it.

Parental Recommendations

StopBullying.gov — Parental Recommendations Government

The U.S. federal resource for understanding, preventing, and responding to cyberbullying, with state law references and reporting guidance.

↗
Parental Recommendations Research Center Research

Academic research, statistics, and up-to-date platform-specific guides for parents on how cyberbullying manifests on each social network.

↗
PACER's National Bullying Prevention Center Advocacy

Resources for parents, educators, and students including action plans, downloadable toolkits, and age-specific prevention guides.

Privacy, Safety & Online Predators

NCMEC CyberTipline Report Abuse

The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children's online reporting tool for child sexual exploitation, grooming, and predatory contact.

↗
FBI — Online Safety for Parents & Caregivers Government

The FBI's guidance on protecting children from online predators, with red flags to watch for and instructions for reporting suspicious contact.

↗
Internet Matters Parent Tool

Step-by-step privacy setting guides for every major platform and device, updated regularly as apps change their interfaces.

↗
ConnectSafely — Parent Guides Parent Tool

Free, concise guides to every major social platform — including privacy tips, age recommendations, and what teens actually do on each app.

↗

Books & Further Reading

The Anxious Generation — Jonathan Haidt Book

A landmark 2024 examination of how the smartphone and social media rewired childhood, with specific policy and parenting recommendations.

↗
It's Complicated — danah boyd Book

Based on years of ethnographic research with American teenagers, this book challenges moral panics while illuminating the real social dynamics of teen online life.

↗
Screenagers — Documentary Film Film

A widely screened documentary exploring the impact of screen time and social media on adolescent development, designed to spark family conversations.

↗
Common Sense Media Research Library Research

Freely accessible annual reports on children's media use, including the definitive survey on teen social media habits and its impacts.
